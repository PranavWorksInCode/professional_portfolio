CK Cake Lounge

Modern e-commerce platform for a custom bakery business

Overview

The CK Cake Lounge website is a comprehensive digital storefront designed to elevate the customer experience. It replaces manual ordering with a streamlined online system, allowing users to browse categories, view detailed product galleries, and submit custom cake requests directly through the platform.

Problem & Solution

Previously, the business relied on phone calls and DMs for orders, leading to lost details and inefficiency. This solution provides a centralized dashboard for managing orders and a user-friendly frontend that works seamlessly across mobile devices, where 80% of their traffic originates.

Tech Stack

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript Bootstrap 5

Key Features

  • Responsive Mobile-First Design
  • Interactive Menu Gallery
  • Custom Order Form Builder
  • Online Consultation Booking
